## Suggested Pricing Models

1. **Monthly SaaS Subscription**
   - Best for small businesses, schools, clinics, restaurants, and shops.
   - Example: Rs. 999 to Rs. 9,999 per month depending on features.

2. **One-Time Setup + Monthly Maintenance**
   - Best for local businesses that want installation, customization, and support.
   - Example: Rs. 15,000 to Rs. 1,50,000 setup plus monthly support.

3. **Per User Pricing**
   - Best for HR, CRM, school, hospital, and office ERP systems.
   - Example: Rs. 99 to Rs. 499 per user per month.

4. **Per Branch Pricing**
   - Best for retail chains, franchises, clinics, restaurants, and warehouses.
   - Example: Rs. 1,999 to Rs. 14,999 per branch per month.

## Recommended First Product

If you want to start quickly, build a **GST Billing + Inventory ERP** first.

Reason:

- Easy to explain to Indian businesses
- Useful for shops, wholesalers, traders, and service providers
- Can be sold locally
- Has recurring subscription potential
- Can later expand into POS, accounting, CRM, and multi-branch ERP
