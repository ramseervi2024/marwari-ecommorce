# Aurbis Workspace Management ERP

## Overview

Aurbis Workspace Management ERP is a comprehensive enterprise platform designed to manage the complete lifecycle of managed office spaces, coworking environments, enterprise workspaces, facility operations, sustainability initiatives, tenant services, and financial operations.

The platform centralizes all business processes into a single integrated system, enabling efficient workspace management, enhanced tenant experiences, and data-driven decision making.

---

# Business Objectives

* Centralized workspace management
* Enterprise tenant lifecycle management
* Facility and maintenance automation
* Smart building integration
* Sustainability and ESG monitoring
* Revenue and billing management
* Enhanced tenant experience through mobile applications
* Real-time analytics and reporting

# Business Logic Summary

Aurbis ERP digitizes the complete lifecycle of managed office spaces, from lead acquisition and tenant onboarding to workspace allocation, billing, facility operations, and sustainability monitoring.

Enterprise tenants can manage employees, visitors, meeting rooms, service requests, and payments through a centralized platform.

Management gains real-time visibility into occupancy, revenue, operational performance, ESG compliance, and workspace utilization across all locations.
